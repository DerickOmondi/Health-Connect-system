Hello, how can we help you today?

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about booking, consultations, and platform security.

Frequently Asked Questions

Access & Account

How do I sign up for a HealthConnect account?

You can create a new account by navigating to the Sign Up page. You will need to provide your first name, last name, email address, and a secure password.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password." You will be prompted to enter your email address, and we will send you a secure link to reset your password.

How do I update my personal information?

Log in and go to your User Dashboard. Select the 'Update Profile' option to modify your contact details and other information.

Appointments & Services

How do I book a new appointment?

Log in and navigate to the Services page or click 'Book Appointment' on your dashboard. You can choose your preferred healthcare professional, consultation type (Video, Audio, or Secure Messaging), and a suitable time slot.

Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes, all appointments can be managed directly from your User Dashboard. Select the appointment you wish to change and choose the 'Reschedule' or 'Cancel' option. Please check our cancellation policy for any fees associated with late cancellations.

What are the available consultation modalities?

HealthConnect supports three modalities:

  • Real-time Secure Video/Audio: For comprehensive virtual consultations.
  • Secure Messaging: Ideal for non-urgent queries and follow-ups with your doctor.

Technical Help

What equipment do I need for a video consultation?

You will need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with a stable internet connection, a working camera, and a microphone. For the best experience, ensure your bandwidth is sufficient for video streaming.

What happens if my internet connection drops during a call?

If your connection is lost, try to reconnect immediately. If the video call cannot be restored, the healthcare professional will attempt to call you on your registered phone number to complete the consultation.

Security and Privacy

How secure is my medical data on HealthConnect?

Your privacy and confidentiality are our highest priority. All data is transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption protocols. Our platform is designed to adhere strictly to the Data Protection and Privacy Acts of Kenya to ensure absolute security of your personal health information.

Can I access my past medical records?

Yes, all your medical records, including consultation notes, lab results, and prescriptions, are securely stored and accessible via your User Dashboard at any time.
