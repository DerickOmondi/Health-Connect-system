Frequently Asked Questions

Access & Account

How do I sign up for a HealthConnect account?

You can create a new account by navigating to the Sign Up page. You will need to provide your first name, last name, email address, and a secure password.

Consultations & Bookings

What happens if I miss my scheduled appointment?

If you miss your appointment, you can reschedule via your Dashboard. Please note our 24-hour cancellation policy for refunds.

Security and Privacy

How secure is my medical data on HealthConnect?

Your privacy and confidentiality are our highest priority. All data is transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption protocols. Our platform adheres strictly to the Data Protection and Privacy Acts of Kenya.
